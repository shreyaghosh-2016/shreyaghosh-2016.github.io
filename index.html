Research Interests

The future of mobility will strive towards environment-friendly, integrated, automated and personalized travel on demand!

To summarize, at this point in my career, my primary interests are in mobility data analytics applied to the field of distributed platform to handle huge data load and utilizing the extracted mobility and semantic knowledge in real-life problems (say, road-safety, unmanned vehicles in large scales and assisting users). I have pursued these interests to date by conducting my PhD research in related domain in different contexts.

In the immediate future I would like to go for building the test-bed, simulator and developing novel ML algorithms, and deploying intelligent mobility-aware systems. However, in the long term I see myself working on expanding my expertise in these areas by a mixture of real-life implementation (self-driving cars/ drones) and empirical work.
